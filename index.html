Plan Overview

This 3-year plan will take me from beginner to advanced full-stack developer with 2-3 hours of daily study.
The roadmap includes:

  • Year 1: HTML, CSS, JavaScript fundamentals, React basics, Git/GitHub
  • Year 2: Advanced React, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, backend development
  • Year 3: Next.js, React Native, advanced concepts, and specialization

Each section includes concepts to master, resources to use, and projects to build.
